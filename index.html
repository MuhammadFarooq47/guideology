What's Included


4-Step Inclusive Guide


Our 4-Part Guides are made specific for each field. They compile all the information students need to be successful during their undergraduate studies, entrance exams, applications, and interviews.

Record Your Progress


The “Your Progress” tabs allow you to record all your progress throughout your 4 years as in undergraduate student. You can record your hours, planned courses, letters, DAT scores, save chosen schools, personal statement, application statuses, and much more.

Extra Services


In the event a student requires additional help with the application/interview process. Guideology offer’s mock interviews, personal statement editing, and personalized school selection.

Support Team


Guideology has assigned a team of students & advisors dedicated to supporting you through this process. Our support team is available to answer any questions or concerns. You can contact your team via live chat or email.

Student Community


Our forums allow students to interact with one another with the goal of making this process easier & more enjoyable. Students can ask questions, share their experiences, and communicate with one another.

Mobile App


The Guideology app allows you to access our program from your mobile device or tablet. You’ll be able record your progress, access your guide, student forums, support team, etc. Same as you would on your computer.

We Are Here To Help

At Guideology we are very familiar with the rigorous path one must follow to achieve their goals of attending their top graduate school. We have witnessed the substantial amount of information one must know to succeed in this process; from taking required courses, acing entrance exams, applying, to interviewing, or even setting a timeline and because of that, Guideology was created.

Mission Statement

Our goal at Guideology is to guide you into your dream graduate school. We’ve worked hard to create inclusive and accurate guides that will cover every step of the path you will take towards your preferred career. Supplemented by our tracking software’s, student forums, support team, and extra services we’ve made that goal possible. We work hard so you and other students can have everything you need all in one place. With an informed staff and a supportive student community we will work hard to overcome this process together.
