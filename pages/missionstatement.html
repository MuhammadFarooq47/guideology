We Are Here To Help

At Guideology we are very familiar with the rigorous path one must follow in order to achieve their goals of attending their top dental, medical, or pharmacy school. We have first-hand experience of what many of you will go through and we are here to help ease the process. We have also witnessed the substantial amount of information one must know in order to succeed in this process; from taking required courses, acing entrance exams, applying, to interviewing, or even setting a timeline and because of that, Guideology was created.

Mission Statement

Our goal at Guideology is to provide an inclusive and accurate guide that will cover every step of the path you will take towards your preferred career. We work hard so you and other students can have everything you need to know all in one place. With an informed staff and a supportive student community we will work hard to overcome this process together.

Saed S. Sayegh

Creator of Guideology
